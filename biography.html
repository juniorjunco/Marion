Biography

Marion Ve

Marion.ve is a Caribbean artist born in Cartagena with a passion for bringing good energy into people's daily lives through vibrant colours, lettering and calligraphy. With over 8 years of experience in the field, he has had the opportunity to take his art both nationally and internationally, leaving his mark in countries such as Germany, Brazil and Mexico. As the director of various projects, he has collaborated with prominent entities such as Pintuco and the Santo Domingo Foundation, as well as other important organisations.

His work has reached various sectors of the city, both private, institutional, informal and social. The central goal of his artistic project is simple but powerful: to invite people to smile from the heart through the use of color, lettering and positive energy. .
