Corporative

Corporative

Thanks to my experience, I have collaborated with several corporate projects that include brands such as Centro de Convenciones Cartagena de Indias, HamburgSUD, Puerto Compas, Hotel Hyatt, Hoteles Estelar, Global Shapers Dusseldorf (Germany-Mexico), Almacenes Éxito, Frisby, Yara, among other organizations. This corporate service is usually requested by human resources departments and consists of relational art workshops and disruptive activities through art and graffiti. These activities allow employees to live a unique experience, where the selected team participates in a co-creation process. This process materializes in a mural full of energy and art, planned, created and produced by themselves under my artistic direction, Marion.ve.

Social

Marion.ve is an artist committed to social responsibility. For more than 4 years, she has been actively collaborating with two foundations in the city: Amigos del Mar and Sonrisas de León. These organizations share the artist's vision and promote change through the education of children.

In addition, he has contributed to various social causes such as Global Shaper (in Mexico, Germany, Colombia and Brazil) and FUNVIVIR (in Mexico, Germany, Colombia and Brazil) and FUNVIVIR, among others. However, his main focus is on the first two foundations mentioned above.

If you have a social project, do not hesitate to get in touch. Marion.ve will be happy to discuss it and explore the possibilities of collaboration.

Sponsorships and support

Artistic management also requires support. Although Marion.ve is an independent artist and thanks his community for allowing him to make a living from art, this project is open to receive and establish commercial, economic, logistical and strategic alliances. The goal is to continue sharing and expanding the culture of Caribbean art and color around the world, taking advantage of a community of more than 23 thousand followers with both local and international presence.

If you are interested in joining us and joining the upcoming activities of the Marion.ve agenda, please do not hesitate to contact us. Together, we can keep adding energy to this great community. Next international project: Spain 2024. If you want to participate, contact us!
